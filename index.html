What & Why

A meal shared from the heart

This is a potluck dinner, not a production, everyone brings a dish, everyone brings themselves, and together it becomes a table. No agenda beyond good food and easy company.

We're doing this because community doesn't happen by accident. It's built one shared meal at a time, one honest conversation at a time. We wanted a night that's light-hearted on the surface and quietly nourishing underneath, replenishment for the soul, disguised as dinner.

How It Will Feel

Easy Breezy

  • 1
    Arrive and settle in. Find a seat, pour something warm, say hello. No rush.
  • 2
    A moment of gratitude. Before we eat, a short pause together, a breath, a few words, nothing formal.
  • 3
    The meal, potluck-style. Plates pass, dishes get shared, seconds are encouraged.
  • 4
    Conversation, unhurried. Real talk, easy laughter, whatever the table wants to be.
  • 5
    A soft close. We wind down together and head home a little fuller, in more ways than one.
